## ğŸ’¡ Usage

### Basic Usage:

```plaintext
.ban <user> [reason]
```

### Parameters:

- **`<user>`**: The member you want to ban. **(Required)**
- **`[reason]`**: Reason for the ban. **_(Optional)_**

### Example:

```plaintext
.ban @user Spamming inappropriate content
```

This will ban `@user` and log "Spamming inappropriate content" as the reason.
